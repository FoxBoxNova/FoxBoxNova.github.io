Unfair Clicker

Description: Unfair Clicker was one of many annual projects that I make for April Fool's Bools. This game is designed to give you anxiety, stress, hurt feelings, a reason to rethink your life revolving around rng, and a good way to practice writing "I will not destroy property after losing a video game". If you like to pretend that you are actually making progress at a casino, then this is the game for you!
